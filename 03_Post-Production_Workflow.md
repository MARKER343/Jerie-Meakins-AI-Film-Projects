# Workflow: Generation

This workflow details the process of using AI tools to generate the visual and audio assets for your film, emphasizing consistency and iterative refinement.

## Objective:
To produce high-quality, consistent video clips and images that align with the pre-production plan, utilizing the established character sheets and prompt libraries.

## Key Activities:

### 1. Initial Scene Generation & Testing
Begin by generating test shots for key scenes to verify that the character prompts and style guidelines are working as intended. This is the time to experiment with different AI models or settings to find the best fit for the project. Focus on achieving the correct look and feel before generating the entire shot list.

### 2. Iterative Prompt Refinement
Based on the initial tests, refine the prompts in your library. Adjust keywords related to lighting, camera angles, and character details to improve consistency. Document any successful prompt combinations or specific negative prompts that help achieve the desired results. This iterative process is crucial for moving from random generation to directed filmmaking.

### 3. Systematic Asset Generation
Once the prompts are refined, systematically work through the shot list or script. Generate multiple variations for each shot to provide options during the editing phase. Pay close attention to character consistency, ensuring that clothing, features, and props remain stable across different angles and scenes.

### 4. Audio & Supplementary Asset Generation
If the project requires AI-generated voiceovers, sound effects, or music, generate these assets concurrently or immediately following the visual generation. Ensure that the audio style matches the visual aesthetic established in pre-production.

## Deliverables:
- A comprehensive library of generated video clips and images, organized by scene or shot number.
- Refined and updated Prompt Library (saved in the `Prompts` directory).
- Generated audio assets (if applicable).

## Notes & Learnings:
Patience is key during the generation phase. Don't settle for the first output; iterate and refine prompts to achieve the best possible result. Keeping detailed notes on which prompts produced which results will save time in future projects.
