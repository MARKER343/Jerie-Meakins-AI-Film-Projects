# Prompt: The Wanderer - Full Body Shot

## Category:
Character

## Core Prompt:
A lone figure, The Wanderer, standing on a windswept dune, looking into the distance.

## Key Elements:
- **Subject:** The Wanderer (as described in `Characters/The_Wanderer.md`)
- **Action/Pose:** Standing confidently, looking into the distance
- **Environment/Setting:** Windswept sand dune, desolate landscape
- **Lighting:** Cinematic lighting, golden hour, warm tones
- **Camera Angle/Shot:** Full body shot, slightly low angle to emphasize scale
- **Style/Aesthetic:** Photorealistic, epic, cinematic film still
- **Details/Modifiers:** Dust particles in the air, long shadows, worn duster coat, staff held loosely

## Negative Prompts (Optional):
blurry, distorted, ugly, cartoon, painting, low resolution, multiple characters

## Example Usage:
`The Wanderer, full body shot, standing on a windswept dune, looking into the distance, cinematic lighting, golden hour, photorealistic, epic, cinematic film still --ar 16:9`

## Notes & Learnings:
Ensure the character description is well-integrated into the prompt to maintain consistency. Experiment with different times of day for varied lighting effects. The `--ar 16:9` aspect ratio is recommended for cinematic feel.
