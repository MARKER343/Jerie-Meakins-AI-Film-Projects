# Prompt: The Rogue - Close-Up

## Category:
Character

## Core Prompt:
Close-up of The Rogue's face, a mischievous grin, eyes sparkling with cunning.

## Key Elements:
- **Subject:** The Rogue (as described in `Characters/The_Rogue.md`)
- **Action/Pose:** Mischievous grin, cunning expression
- **Environment/Setting:** Dimly lit alley, soft glow from a streetlamp in the background
- **Lighting:** Soft rim lighting, dramatic shadows on one side of the face
- **Camera Angle/Shot:** Close-up, eye-level
- **Style/Aesthetic:** Gritty realism, film noir influence, high contrast
- **Details/Modifiers:** Slight glint in her hazel eyes, a few strands of hair falling across her face, subtle texture on leather vest collar

## Negative Prompts (Optional):
blurry, distorted, ugly, cartoon, painting, low resolution, unnatural expression, too much light

## Example Usage:
`Close-up of The Rogue's face, mischievous grin, eyes sparkling with cunning, soft rim lighting, dimly lit alley, gritty realism, film noir --ar 3:2`

## Notes & Learnings:
Focus on facial expressions and subtle lighting to convey personality. The `3:2` aspect ratio can be effective for portrait-style close-ups. Ensure consistency with the character's established features.
