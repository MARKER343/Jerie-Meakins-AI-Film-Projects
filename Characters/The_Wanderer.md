# Character: The Wanderer

This template helps in creating consistent and reusable AI characters for your filmmaking projects. Fill out the sections below to define your character's visual and narrative attributes.

## Character Name: The Wanderer

## Core Concept:
A lone figure traversing desolate landscapes, searching for remnants of a lost civilization. Stoic, resilient, and burdened by a mysterious past.

## Visual Description:

### General Appearance:
- **Age:** Late 30s
- **Gender:** Male
- **Ethnicity/Race:** Ambiguous, weathered features
- **Body Type:** Lean, athletic, slightly gaunt
- **Height:** Tall
- **Distinguishing Features:** Deep-set eyes, a faint scar across his left cheekbone, worn leather bracer on his right forearm.

### Face:
- **Hair:** Medium length, dark brown, often disheveled, streaked with dust.
- **Eyes:** Piercing grey, often scanning the horizon, conveying a sense of perpetual vigilance.
- **Nose:** Straight, slightly prominent.
- **Mouth/Lips:** Thin, usually set in a grim line.
- **Facial Hair (if applicable):** Short, neatly trimmed beard, dusty.

### Clothing/Costume:
- **Primary Outfit:** A heavy, dark grey duster coat, layered over a faded, dark green tunic. Durable, patched-up cargo pants and sturdy, high-laced boots.
- **Accessories:** A tarnished silver compass worn around his neck, a utility belt with pouches and a canteen, fingerless gloves.
- **Color Palette:** Muted earth tones, dark greys, faded greens, with occasional hints of rust or dried blood.

### Silhouette:
Tall and slender, with the duster coat adding a broad, flowing outline. The silhouette often appears solitary and imposing against open skies.

## Personality & Demeanor:
Quiet, observant, and cautious. Rarely speaks unless necessary. Carries a sense of profound melancholy but possesses an unwavering resolve. Distrustful of strangers but not inherently hostile.

## Backstory (brief):
A former cartographer from a fallen empire, now wandering the ruins, seeking a way to reverse the cataclysm that destroyed his world.

## Key Props/Items:
A custom-made, ornate staff that doubles as a walking stick and a defensive weapon. A leather-bound journal filled with cryptic maps and notes.

## Reference Images/Style Guides (Optional):
- Post-apocalyptic desert wanderer aesthetics (e.g., Mad Max, Dune)
- Stoic lone wolf archetypes
