# Character: The Rogue

## Character Name: The Rogue

## Core Concept:
A quick-witted and agile individual, living on the fringes of society. Resourceful, charming, and always looking for an opportunity, but with a hidden moral compass.

## Visual Description:

### General Appearance:
- **Age:** Early 20s
- **Gender:** Female
- **Ethnicity/Race:** Mediterranean
- **Body Type:** Lithe, athletic, flexible
- **Height:** Average
- **Distinguishing Features:** A mischievous grin, a small, intricate tattoo of a bird on her wrist, quick, darting eyes.

### Face:
- **Hair:** Short, dark, choppy bob, often with a few strands falling across her face.
- **Eyes:** Bright, intelligent hazel, full of curiosity and a hint of mischief.
- **Nose:** Small, slightly upturned.
- **Mouth/Lips:** Full, often curved into a smirk or a playful smile.
- **Facial Hair (if applicable):** N/A

### Clothing/Costume:
- **Primary Outfit:** Dark, fitted leather vest over a loose, off-white linen shirt. Dark, practical trousers tucked into soft, knee-high boots. A utility belt with small pouches and hidden compartments.
- **Accessories:** Several thin, silver rings on her fingers, a leather bracer on her left forearm, a small, ornate dagger sheathed at her hip.
- **Color Palette:** Dark blues, deep greens, earthy browns, with metallic accents from her accessories.

### Silhouette:
Compact and agile, with a slight, almost bird-like posture. Her movements are fluid and quick, giving her a dynamic silhouette even when still.

## Personality & Demeanor:
Confident, playful, and quick to adapt. She uses humor and charm to disarm others but is fiercely independent. Has a strong sense of justice, though she often operates outside the law.

## Backstory (brief):
Orphaned at a young age, she learned to survive on the streets, developing a keen eye for detail and a knack for getting into (and out of) trouble.

## Key Props/Items:
A set of lock-picking tools, a small, intricately carved wooden bird, and a pouch filled with various currencies and trinkets.
